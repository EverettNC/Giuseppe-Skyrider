## 🎭 Tone Checklist

Before you hit send, verify:

- [ ] Professional but authentic (your voice, not corporate speak)
- [ ] Metrics-driven (numbers matter)
- [ ] Value-focused (what's in it for them)
- [ ] Respectful of their time (clear ask, clear value)
- [ ] Personal without oversharing (your story serves the mission)
- [ ] Confident without arrogance (you're offering partnership, not asking for favors)

---

## ⚠️ What NOT to Do

❌ **Don't:**
- Lead with "I'm a huge fan" (they know, get to value)
- Apologize for reaching out (you have legitimate business value)
- Undersell your impact (1000+ conversions is REAL)
- Send without proofreading (one typo in a pitch to the CEO is one too many)
- Oversell/exaggerate (honesty is your brand, protect it)
- Make demands (this is partnership proposal, not ultimatum)

✅ **Do:**
- Lead with impact metrics
- Show respect for their time
- Prove you understand their values
- Demonstrate you've done the work
- Make it easy to say yes (clear next step)
- Follow up once if no response (they're busy, be persistent not pushy)
